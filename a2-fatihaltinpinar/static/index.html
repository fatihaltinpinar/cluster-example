IF THESE TREES COULD TALK

What do they have to say?

Personally I don't like changes. I like doing the same thing over and over again. Just because I like it. Maybe change is a really important thing for you but not for me, especially in songs that I listen to. I like listening the same thing over and over again. Maybe the reason behind this is that I like instrumental music. I feel different every time I listen to, like it is a deeper level, another chapter, another page...

I met this group back in March 2015 with the song "Barren Lands Of the Modern Dinosaur". I added it to my Spotify playlist then left it there for a while. I was listening to random things back then. Let's say I did not have a music taste. :)
